Dr. Sripati Jana

Scientist · EPFL - Swiss Federal Institute of Technology Lausanne

I am a research scientist working at the forefront of sustainable catalysis and organic synthesis, with expertise encompassing asymmetric catalysis, chiral catalyst design, photochemistry and photocatalysis, transition-metal catalysis, and computational chemistry. My work advances mechanism-guided reaction development, integrating experiment and theory to address complex synthetic challenges. I currently pursue this research at EPFL in the group of Prof. Nicolai Cramer, following doctoral studies with Prof. René M. Koenigs at RWTH Aachen University.
